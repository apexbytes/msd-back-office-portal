System Overview

Musondosi is a multi-vertical classifieds and listing platform. As an administrator or support agent, your primary responsibility is to ensure the safety, quality, and smooth operation of the platform.

SUPERADMIN Role

Has unrestricted access. Can manage security settings, system logs, create new roles, and assign permissions to other staff members.

SUPPORT Role

Focuses on user assistance. Can moderate listings, reply to support tickets, and assist users with subscription issues.

Navigating the Portal

User Management

Found under the Users tab. Here you can view all registered users, their verification status, and their online activity. You can manually unlock accounts that have been locked due to too many failed login attempts, or ban/deactivate malicious users.

Listings (Properties & Vehicles)

Found under the Properties and Vehicles tabs. This is where you moderate user-submitted content. You can toggle the "Published" state of a listing, mark listings as "Featured" so they appear on the homepage, or delete listings that violate our terms of service. For vehicles, you also manage the Makes & Models taxonomies.

Monetization (Adverts & Subscriptions)

Found under the Adverts and Subscriptions tabs. Adverts are banner ads placed around the public site. Subscriptions dictate how many listings a user can post. If a user pays offline, you can manually "Grant" or "Override" their subscription tier from here.

Support Tickets

Found under the Tickets tab. When users submit contact forms or help requests, they appear here. You can view the conversation thread, reply directly to the user's email, and update the ticket status (e.g., Open, In Progress, Resolved).

Marketing (Testimonials & Partners)

Found under the Testimonials and Partners tabs. These control the content shown on the public landing page. Add client logos to Partners, and highlight glowing reviews in the Testimonials section.

Security Best Practices

  • Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA): Go to your Profile Settings and enable MFA immediately to protect your staff account.
  • Save Backup Codes: When generating MFA Backup Codes, save them securely offline. Do not store them on your desktop.
  • Log Out: The system has an automatic idle timeout, but you should always manually log out when stepping away from a shared or public computer.
  • Verify Users: Before making account changes or overriding subscriptions on behalf of a user, always verify their identity via their registered email address.
